GPBeam

Auto-offloads your GoPro to a drive the moment you plug it in.

This is the M1 build — the engine is live and runs from the menu-bar / tray. The full settings UI (profiles, cloud, filters, history) arrives in M3.

For now, the destination is read from the GPBEAM_DEST environment variable (default: ~/GPBeam). Plug a GoPro in SD / storage mode (or use a card reader) and footage copies automatically.
